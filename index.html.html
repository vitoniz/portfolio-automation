/// Under the hood

What generate_invoices.py actually does.

01

Reads the spreadsheet

Pandas loads the CSV export and groups every row by invoice number, so multi-line invoices stay together.

02

Builds each invoice

For every client, it lays out a formatted PDF: header, billing details, itemized table, subtotal and total.

03

Saves it, ready to send

Every PDF drops into an /output folder, named by invoice number — no manual formatting, no copy-paste.
